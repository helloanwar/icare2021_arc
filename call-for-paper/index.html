Call for paper

ICARE2021 invites scholars, researchers, practitioners and students to submit papers for virtual paper presentation and to join virtual talks and colloquia addressing this year’s themes and topics. If you are unable to attend the conference, you may still join the community and submit your article for peer review and possible publication.

Scope & Benefit

All selected authors will be offered for publication in Scopus indexed and other relevant journals, subject to the compliance with journal’s guidelines, terms and conditions.


Five (5) excellent papers will be selected for the Best Paper Award.

Step 2 : Submission of Full Paper

  1. Please register and upload the abstract in Step 1.
  2. Within 7 days, you will receive a notification of acceptance (approved abstract)
  3. Then you may proceed to submit the full paper to be peer reviewed.
  4. All submitted papers will be peer-reviewed.
  5. Click “submit full paper” button to proceed

Online Full paper Submission System

Click the button below

Submit Full Paper 

Paper submission should be in MS Word format. Times New Roman 11-point font, single-spacing with maximum 5 pages[snms1] . Please clearly state the name (s), affiliation, contact number and of author(s) on the first page. The abstract shall not exceed 200 words and should provide the statement of the purpose and method of the study, including significant conclusions of the research. Figures, tables and references should be included on separate pages at the end of the text. Please keep endnotes and footnotes to a minimum.
